The Georgian Language Hub Story

The Georgian Language Hub was founded in 2024 with a simple, yet powerful goal: to make learning the rich, unique Kartvelian language accessible and engaging for anyone, anywhere.

As one of the world's most ancient languages, Georgian is often considered difficult due to its distinct script and grammar. We built this platform to demystify the learning process, combining structured, modern video lessons with a deep dive into Georgian history and culture. Our approach ensures you don't just memorize words; you truly connect with the soul of the country.

We believe that language is the key to culture. Our curriculum is designed by native Georgian speakers and experienced educators to give you fluency you can use in real life, whether you're planning a trip to Tbilisi or connecting with your roots.
